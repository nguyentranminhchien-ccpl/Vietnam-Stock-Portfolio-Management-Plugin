## 📋 Report Structure

Produce the report in the following eight sections. For each section, the guiding questions are prompts for your analysis — **do not answer them as a list; weave them into cohesive prose**.

---

### 1. Executive Summary *(~200 words)*

- What does [COMPANY NAME] do and where does it sit in the [INDUSTRY] value chain?
- **State your investment rating** (Strong Buy / Buy / Hold / Sell / Strong Sell) and a **12-month price target** upfront.
- What are the 2 strongest catalysts and the 2 most critical risks?

---

### 2. Financial Performance & Health *(5 years + TTM)*

**Income Statement**
- Revenue CAGR over the period. Is growth accelerating or decelerating?
- Gross margin, EBIT margin, and net margin trends — what is driving compression or expansion?
- Any one-off items distorting reported earnings?

**Balance Sheet**
- Net debt / EBITDA and interest coverage ratio trend.
- Current ratio and quick ratio — any liquidity stress?
- Working capital cycle (days inventory, days receivable, days payable) vs. sector norms.

**Cash Flow**
- FCF conversion rate (FCF / Net Income). Is the company a reliable cash generator?
- Capex intensity — maintenance vs. growth capex split if discernible.
- Any red flags (e.g., operating cash flow consistently below net income)?

---

**Interpretation**

**Conclusion**: Overvalued, undervalued, or fairly priced? Why?

---

### 4. Business Model & Competitive Moat

- Core segments and their % contribution to revenue and EBIT.
- What is the **primary source** of the company's moat (e.g., brand, patents, network effects, cost leadership, regulatory advantages, distribution scale)? How durable is it over a 5-year horizon?
- Key vulnerabilities to the moat.

---

### 5. Growth Strategy & Future Outlook

- What specific initiatives is management pursuing for the next 3–5 years (capacity expansion, new products, geographic expansion, M&A)?
- TAM sizing: relevant market size and the company's market share trajectory.
- Key macro or industry tailwinds/headwinds affecting the growth outlook.

---

### 6. Management & Governance

- CEO tenure and relevant track record. Any recent leadership changes?
- **Capital allocation scorecard**: Has management created or destroyed value through M&A, capex, or dividends over the last 5 years?
- Insider/major shareholder ownership percentage and its implications for minority shareholders.
- Any related-party transaction concerns?

---

### 7. Risk Analysis

**Company-Specific Risks (Idiosyncratic)**
List the top 3, each with: *Description → Likelihood (Low/Med/High) → Potential Impact (Low/Med/High) → Mitigant*.

**Macro / Market Risks (Systemic)**
List the top 3 using the same format.

---

### 8. Final Investment Recommendation

- Reiterate your **rating and 12-month price target**.
- Summarize the key variables an investor should monitor going forward.
- State the **single most important reason** to own or avoid the stock at today's price.
